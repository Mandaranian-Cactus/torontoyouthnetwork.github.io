FinHacks 2019

November 30 2019 - Centre for Social Innovation, Toronto

What is FinHacks?

FinHacks is a fintech hackathon organized by Toronto Youth Network and Target Alpha that will bring 100 students together at the Centre for Social Innovation- Spadina Lounge to build something using the latest technologies.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I start working on my hack before the event?

No, students should not be working on their projects before FinHacks and we do not allow participants to work on pre-existing projects.

When and where is FinHacks?

FinHacks is on Saturday November 30, 2019 from 9am to 5 pm at the Centre for Social Innovation- Spadina Lounge

Who can participate?

Any high school or university student can participate

How many people can be on a team?

A maximum of 5 people can be on a team

How much does it cost to attend FinHacks?

FinHacks is free to attend! We will provide food as well.

What if I've never been to a hackathon before?

FinHacks welcomes students of all skill levels and hackathons are great place to learn new skills in a short amount of time.
